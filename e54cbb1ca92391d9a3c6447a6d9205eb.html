Honor 7A 2/16GB smartphone, gold

89 325

Huawei Honor 7A is a smartphone with a modern design. On the front side there is a strong and beautiful 2.5 D glass. The back side is matte, and it is implemented from plastic. The Honor 7A features a 5.45-inch FullView display with a resolution of 1440x720. The Honor 7A smartphone is equipped with a 4-core Mediatek MT6739 processor. This is where the kernels are used Cortex-A53, which operate at a maximum frequency of 1500 MHz. The device contains the PowerVR GE8100 graphics accelerator. Built-in memory of 16 GB and 2 GB of RAM, and you can expand the internal storage with memory cards up to 256 GB. Android 8.1 acts as an operating system. At the same time, the interface is upgraded thanks to branded shell EMUI 8.1. Inside the device is a non-removable battery with a capacity of 3020 mAh. Huawei Honor 7A supports two Nano-SIM simultaneously. In this case, the memory card can be installed separately. There is a built-in FM radio, as well as an LTE module. The Huawei Honor 7A has a main camera with phase autofocus. She it received a 13-megapixel sensor, with a fast focus function. There is a powerful flash and HD-resolution, allowing you to take clear and bright photos. On the front of the smartphone is a front-facing 5-megapixel camera with LED flash. This allows you to make excellent self-portraits at any time of the day. The smartphone is certified by the EAC and has a Russian-language menu interface, as well as a"User Manual".
